About Bell Studios

A small studio built to follow good ideas wherever they lead.

Bell Studios creates original products across publishing and software, then occasionally applies the same thinking to unusual collaborations with organizations and communities.

Not an agency. Not one lane.

Bell Studios began with a simple impulse: notice something that could work better, then build the version that should exist.

That has led to family puzzle apps, an emotion-first AI companion, a 300-page road trip activity book, sponsor-funded sports programs, and Little Locals — a city welcome book designed for children moving into a new community.

The medium changes. The approach does not: make the idea clear, useful, thoughtful, and complete enough to earn a place in someone’s life.

What we build

Products first.

Most of our work begins as an original Bell Studios product. We also develop custom programs when the right partner and the right problem line up.

Publishing

Original activity books and repeatable publishing systems for families, communities, and organizations.

Software

Focused consumer apps designed around a clear job, simple interaction, and long-term usefulness.

Collaborations

Selected one-off projects that benefit from product strategy, creative development, software, or systems thinking.

Built independently in North Texas.
