Frequently Asked Questions

The practical details.

How many books will be distributed?

The first Celina edition is planned around an initial print run of approximately 1,000 copies. Books will be replenished among participating distribution partners, and additional copies may be printed while the edition remains active.

Is this an advertisement or a sponsorship?

It is a sponsorship opportunity that includes branded promotional placement. Sponsors help fund a free resource for local families while receiving visibility inside the book.

How long does the placement last?

Sponsorship applies to the current Celina edition rather than a fixed number of days. The edition remains in circulation until it is replaced by a future edition.

Can two businesses from the same category participate?

Custom activity sponsorships are intended to be category-exclusive. Resource and coupon placements may be organized differently depending on the page and business type.

Who creates the sponsored activity?

Bell Studios develops the concept and page design with input from the sponsor. Final sponsor branding, offer details, and QR destination are approved before printing.

Can sponsors distribute copies too?

Yes. Subject to inventory, sponsors may receive copies to share with families at their own location or community events.
