Little Locals: Celina

Reach new families as they arrive.

Little Locals is a free children’s activity book distributed through home builders, realtors, apartments, and welcome partners — with a limited group of local businesses helping make it possible.

View sponsorshipsSee example pages

The idea

A welcome gift families will actually use.

Each 64-page edition combines puzzles, coloring, local facts, and family activities with thoughtfully integrated sponsor placements. The initial Celina run is planned at approximately 1,000 copies, with replenishment based on distribution demand.

Delivered at the right moment

Families receive the book while they are choosing dentists, vets, restaurants, classes, home services, and other local favorites.

Made to be kept

This is not a postcard or fleeting digital impression. Children use the pages and parents keep the book nearby.

Limited local participation

Featured activity pages can include category exclusivity, so a sponsor is not placed beside a direct competitor.

Concept examples

The sponsor becomes part of the page.

Featured sponsors receive a custom activity built around their industry, plus branding, contact details, and a clear call to action for parents.

Founding opportunities

Choose the level that fits.

Celina is the first edition. Founding sponsors help establish the program and receive first consideration when their category becomes available for renewal.

Questions

What businesses usually ask.

Who receives the books?

New and relocating families through participating home builders, realtors, apartments, sponsors, and other local welcome partners.

How many copies are planned?

The initial print run is expected to be approximately 1,000 copies. Additional copies may be printed as distribution partners use their inventory.

How long does a sponsorship last?

Sponsorship applies to the current Celina edition. Existing sponsors receive the first opportunity to renew before their category is offered elsewhere.

Can more than one business from the same category participate?

Featured activity pages are generally category-exclusive. Resource and coupon placements may be handled more flexibly depending on the category.
