Privacy Policy

PUZL is designed to be simple, safe, and respectful of user privacy.

Information We Collect

PUZL does not require you to create an account and does not ask for personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the app may use third-party services (such as advertising providers or analytics tools) that may collect limited, non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • General usage data (e.g., app interactions, crash reports)
  • Advertising identifiers (if ads are enabled)

This information is used solely to operate, maintain, and improve the app experience.

Advertising

PUZL may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These providers may collect and use limited device information to serve ads.

PUZL does not knowingly collect or store personal data and does not sell user data.

Children’s Privacy

PUZL is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
