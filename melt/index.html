Emotion-first clarity

MELT helps tangled thoughts make sense.

A private AI companion that meets you where you are, avoids therapy-speak, and helps surface the patterns underneath what you feel.

Clarity without the clinical tone.

MELT is not therapy, but it is designed to be therapeutic. It translates your thoughts back to you in clearer language and adapts the depth of the conversation to what you are ready for.

No fixing. No agenda. No requirement to perform a certain kind of self-help. Your conversations remain private, and MELT does not even ask for your name.

MELT is not therapy and cannot handle emergencies. Contact local emergency services or an appropriate crisis resource if you need immediate help.
